What makes Locksmith different?

We built a new standard for accounts that breaks the trade-offs in existing smart wallets. Locksmith is fully on-chain, 100% decentralized, and easy to integrate with any dApp.

Do I have to download anything?

Nope! Just connect with your wallet of choice and deposit your crypto into secure smart accounts. Add other wallets later to access your crypto from anywhere.

Why is Locksmith safer than my current wallet?

Locksmith lets you deposit your crypto into an account connected to your wallet, but not directly inside it. If your wallet gets drained your crypto is still safe. And if you lose your wallet, Locksmith can reconnect to a new one.
